A little about me

I was born in 1975 and have been working in accounting since 1998 after completing my vocational training. Since 2009 I am an independent counseling center manager of a tax office. However, I have always been interested in IT and website development and therefore decided in 2019 to complete a further education as a full-stack web developer at the online institute Altcademy. The goal is a complete career change to web development. I am married, have two dogs and spend my free time hiking, photography and motorcycling.
